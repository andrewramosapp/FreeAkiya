## Description (4000 chars max)
Find and buy affordable vacant homes in Japan — from as little as $500.

Japan has over 9 million abandoned properties (akiya) sitting empty across the country. CheapAkiya brings them all into one place, translated into English, enriched with everything you need to make a real decision.

**Browse thousands of listings**
Scroll through real akiya from municipal banks, prefectural portals, and Japan's top property sites. Filter by region, price, condition, and subsidy availability. Every listing is translated and enriched — no Japanese required.

**Explore the map**
See all listings across Japan on an interactive map. Zoom into any city to see individual properties. Tap a cluster to browse homes in that area.

**Save your favourites**
Heart any listing to save it. Your saved homes sync across devices when you're signed in.

**Government subsidies**
Hundreds of listings qualify for Japanese government relocation subsidies — sometimes up to ¥3,000,000. CheapAkiya surfaces these automatically so you never miss a deal.

**Contact directly**
Send inquiries to CheapAkiya from inside the app. Premium members get direct contact details for sellers.

**Free & Premium**
- Free: Browse all listings, save homes, view photos and location
- Premium ($12/mo): Unlock full listing details, station distance, disaster risk scores, fiber internet data, and direct seller contact info

Whether you're dreaming of retiring to rural Japan, looking for a remote work base, or just want a cheap piece of real estate — CheapAkiya is where you start.
